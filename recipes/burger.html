burger recipe

Description

A hamburger, or simply a burger, is a dish consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat,
typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll.

Ingredients

  • beef
  • onion
  • egg

  • rolled oats
  • bread
